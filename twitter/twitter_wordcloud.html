WordCloud

WordCloud is used to display text data visually. By using WordCloud, the frequency of words can be displayed in an interesting form but still informative. More often a word is used, bigger the size of the word is displayed in WordCloud.

Function used for making WordCloud is wordcloud(). There are several parameter usually used in that function: - data : the text contain words that will be made wordCloud - min.freq : minimum frequency of words to be displayed - max.words : maximum words that will be displayed - random.order : words that have large frequencies will be plotted near the center - colors : coloring WordCloud

library(tokenizers)
tweets_text <- tweets_text %>% 
                    tokenize_words() %>% 
                         as.character()

library(RColorBrewer)
library(wordcloud)
wordcloud(tweets_text,
         min.freq=1, 
         max.words = 80, 
         random.order = FALSE, 
         colors=brewer.pal(7, "Set2"))
